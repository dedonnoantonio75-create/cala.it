Getting Here

Directions to Cala dei Balcani

✈

From Brindisi Airport

Follow the SS613 towards Lecce, then take the SP365 towards Maglie and SP358 towards Santa Cesarea Terme. Journey time approximately 1 hour 10 minutes (90 km). Satnav: Via Roma 5, Santa Cesarea Terme.

🚂

From Lecce Station

From Lecce, take the SP365 south towards Maglie, then follow the SP358 east towards the coast. Santa Cesarea Terme is well signposted from Maglie. Journey approximately 50 minutes (45 km).

🚗

Parking & Transfers

Free parking is available at the venue. For guests requiring transfers from airports or hotels, we can recommend reliable private transfer services operating in the area. Please request details when you contact us.
