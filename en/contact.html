Find Us

Santa Cesarea Terme is located on the eastern coast of Salento, approximately 40 km south of Lecce. The nearest airports are Brindisi (BDS, approx. 90 km) and Bari (BRI, approx. 180 km). From Lecce, take the SS16 Adriatica southward.
