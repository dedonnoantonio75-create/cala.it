Every Wedding is a Masterpiece

At Cala dei Balcani, we believe that your wedding should be as unique as your love story. Since 2003, we have had the privilege of hosting couples from across Europe and beyond, each celebration crafted with meticulous attention to detail and a deep passion for creating unforgettable memories. Whether you envision an intimate ceremony for a small gathering of close friends and family, or a grand celebration beneath the stars with the Adriatic shimmering below, we have the expertise and the setting to bring your vision to life.

Official Ceremonies

Civil Ceremonies by the Sea

Cala dei Balcani hosts official civil ceremonies on-site with full legal recognition in Italy. The venue works closely with local authorities in Santa Cesarea Terme to facilitate civil marriage ceremonies for both Italian and international couples, handling all administrative coordination so you can focus entirely on your day. The ceremony space is positioned to capture the magnificent sea view, with the ancient Saracen Tower visible on the horizon. Seasonal white flowers — roses, orchids, peonies — are arranged to complement the natural surroundings without competing with them.

  • Official civil ceremony with full Italian legal recognition
  • Multilingual ceremony support (English, French, German, Spanish)
  • Coordination with Santa Cesarea Terme municipal offices
  • Personalised floral arrangements in white and ivory
  • Dedicated wedding coordinator from first contact to farewell
  • Professional sound system for music and vows

The Celebration

Receptions at Sunset

As the sun descends over the Salentine hills and the Adriatic takes on its evening hues of gold and rose, the reception begins. The Saracen Tower, floodlit in cobalt blue, becomes the iconic backdrop against which your celebration unfolds. Long tables dressed in white linen, crystal glassware catching the last light, the scent of jasmine on the warm evening air — this is the stage we set for your most important evening.

The menu, crafted from the finest local ingredients, is designed to reflect the authentic flavours of Puglia while meeting the highest standards of contemporary fine dining. Octopus with aged caciocavallo podolico, Mediterranean prawns dressed with the purest local olive oil, hand-dried friselle with ripe Salentine tomatoes — each course tells the story of this extraordinary land. Dancing continues under the stars as the tower glows blue in the Adriatic night, a sight your guests will carry with them for the rest of their lives.
