The Founder

Maria Cristina Urso —
The Visionary Founder

In 2003, Maria Cristina Urso transformed a spectacular stretch of Salentine coastline into one of Italy's most exclusive wedding destinations. A native of Puglia with a lifelong passion for her region's natural beauty and cultural heritage, she envisioned a place where couples from around the world could celebrate love against a backdrop of ancient history and breathtaking scenery.

Her dedication to authentic Salentine hospitality — combining world-class service with the genuine warmth of southern Italy — has made Cala dei Balcani a byword for excellence in luxury events. Every detail, from the local stone pathways to the hand-selected ceramics, reflects Maria Cristina's commitment to preserving and celebrating the unique character of Salento. She understood instinctively that true luxury is not merely about grandeur: it is about meaning, memory, and the irreplaceable sense of place.

Over two decades, her tireless work has earned Cala dei Balcani an international reputation, welcoming couples from across Europe, the Americas, and beyond to the shores of the Adriatic. Yet even today, Maria Cristina greets each couple as she always has — with personal warmth, genuine curiosity, and an unwavering determination to make their celebration extraordinary.

The Director

Francesco Urso —
Director & Guardian of Excellence

Francesco Urso brings a new generation's energy and vision to the venue his mother founded. As Director, Francesco oversees every aspect of operations at Cala dei Balcani, ensuring that each celebration meets the exacting standards that have defined the venue for over two decades. His deep knowledge of the territory — the ancient olive groves, the crystalline coves, the traditions of Salentine cuisine — informs every decision he makes.

Francesco works closely with couples from the moment of first contact through to the final farewell, personally guaranteeing that no detail is overlooked and that every wedding reflects the couple's unique story. He collaborates with the region's finest artisans, producers, and creatives to source ingredients, flowers, music, and décor that are always rooted in the authentic spirit of Salento — never generic, never formulaic, always deeply personal.

His commitment to excellence is not merely professional: it is a continuation of a family legacy built on love for this extraordinary place. When you choose Cala dei Balcani, you do not simply book a venue — you become part of a story that began in 2003 and continues to unfold with every celebration on these ancient shores.

The Setting

Santa Cesarea Terme & the Adriatic Coast

Cala dei Balcani stands above the sea in Santa Cesarea Terme, one of the most beautiful and historically resonant towns on the Salentine coast. The setting is defined by the dramatic limestone cliffs, the extraordinary clarity of the Adriatic waters below, and the long horizon that ends, on clear days, at the mountains of Albania — a panorama that has given this place its evocative name.

The venue's heart is the Torre Saracena di Monte Saracino, a watchtower built in the 16th century following the devastating Ottoman raid on Otranto in 1480. Constructed in the distinctive truncated-cone form typical of the Salentine defensive towers — nine metres in diameter, ten metres in height — it has stood for over five hundred years as a guardian of this coastline. Today it stands as a symbol of something altogether different: endurance, beauty, and the celebration of life.

La Torre Saracena S.r.l., founded by the Urso family, operates Cala dei Balcani with a deep sense of stewardship toward this heritage. Every event held here is designed to honour rather than overwhelm its extraordinary setting — creating celebrations that feel as timeless as the landscape itself.
