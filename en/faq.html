Planning Made Simple

Planning a wedding in Italy from abroad — or simply exploring a venue for the first time — naturally raises many questions. All Your Questions

Cala dei Balcani can comfortably accommodate between 30 and 250 guests for a seated reception on the panoramic terrace. For intimate ceremonies of fewer than 30 people, the garden setting is particularly atmospheric. We recommend contacting us to discuss your specific requirements, as the ideal configuration depends on the style of event you envision — long banquet tables, clusters of rounds, a garden cocktail reception, or a combination of spaces.

Yes. Cala dei Balcani is an authorised venue for civil weddings under Italian law, in collaboration with the Municipality of Santa Cesarea Terme. This means international couples can marry legally in Italy at our venue. We work with experienced bilingual officiants and legal advisors who can guide you through the documentation requirements for your specific nationality.

For symbolic ceremonies — which are not legally binding but deeply personal — there are no bureaucratic requirements, and the ceremony can be designed entirely to your wishes, beliefs, and traditions.

We provide our own in-house catering team, sourcing exclusively from local Salentine producers. Our cuisine is built around fresh, seasonal, km-zero ingredients — Adriatic seafood, masseria cheeses, local vegetables, and Salentine wines. Custom menus are designed in close collaboration with each couple, including a dedicated tasting session prior to the event.

External caterers are not permitted, as our culinary offering is central to the identity and quality standards of the venue. However, external pastry chefs and wedding cake makers can be accommodated with prior agreement.

The main terrace and reception areas are accessible at ground level. The venue is situated on a coastal cliff, and some areas of the garden involve uneven stone surfaces or gentle slopes inherent to the natural landscape. We recommend contacting us in advance to discuss the specific needs of any guests with mobility impairments so we can ensure appropriate arrangements — including reserved seating, assistance, and designated accessible routes — are in place well before the event.

The nearest international airport is Brindisi (BDS), approximately 90 km away — around 1 hour 10 minutes by car. Bari Airport (BRI) is approximately 175 km away, around 2 hours by car. From Lecce railway station, the venue is approximately 45 km, about 50 minutes by car via the SP365 and SP358.

We can recommend reliable private transfer companies for guests who need transportation from airports or stations. The venue address for satellite navigation is: Via Roma 5, 73020 Santa Cesarea Terme (LE), Puglia.

Santa Cesarea Terme and its surroundings offer a wide range of accommodation, from historic boutique hotels and converted masserie to private villas and quality agriturismi. The town itself — famous for its thermal spas and Moorish-influenced Liberty architecture — provides an exceptional stay for guests who wish to extend their visit to Salento.

We provide all couples with a curated recommended accommodation list to share with their guests, and can assist in coordinating group bookings at selected properties in the area.

The Salentine coast enjoys excellent weather from May through October, and rain during this period is uncommon. However, every event at Cala dei Balcani has a detailed weather contingency plan prepared in advance. The venue has covered and sheltered areas that can accommodate the full ceremony and reception programme should conditions require it.

We monitor weather forecasts closely in the days before each event and communicate our contingency arrangements clearly to every couple and their wedding planner well in advance, so there are no surprises. The contingency spaces maintain the atmosphere and aesthetic of the main terrace and garden.

Yes, absolutely. We warmly welcome external photographers, videographers, musicians, DJs, florists, hair and make-up artists, and other creative professionals. The only exception is catering, which is exclusively managed in-house to maintain our quality standards.

We recommend sharing our venue information pack with your suppliers ahead of time. All external professionals must coordinate their setup and access timing with our events team. We have excellent working relationships with many Salento-based wedding professionals and are happy to provide recommendations if you are looking for trusted local talent.

We do not publish fixed pricing publicly, as every event is unique and costed individually based on the date, number of guests, menu choices, and services required. We invite you to contact us via the enquiry form on our Contact page, by email at info@caladeibalcani.com, or by telephone at +39 334 998 5447.

We prefer to have a conversation first — whether by telephone, video call, or in person — to understand your vision before presenting a detailed, tailored proposal. This ensures the quotation we provide reflects exactly what you have in mind.

We strongly encourage all couples to visit Cala dei Balcani in person before booking. No gallery or description fully captures the experience of standing on the terrace and seeing the Adriatic stretched out before you. To arrange a visit, contact us via the enquiry form, by email, or by telephone. We will suggest dates that work for you and ensure a member of our team is available for a personal guided tour of every space.

For couples based abroad, we also offer video consultations via Zoom, Teams, or WhatsApp — a wonderful way to see the venue, meet our team, and ask all your questions before planning your trip to Salento.

When you book Cala dei Balcani for your wedding or event, you have full and exclusive use of the entire venue — the panoramic terrace, the Mediterranean garden, the Saracen Tower surroundings, and all supporting facilities. There will be no other events, ceremonies, or public access during your celebration. We hold only one event per day.

We believe exclusivity is fundamental to the intimacy and magic of a wedding day. This is your day, and this is your place.

The wedding season runs from May to October. June, July, and September are the most popular months — reliably warm and sunny weather, the sea at its most inviting, and the light in the late afternoon and evening is exceptional for photography. August is ideal for those who love the full intensity of the southern Italian summer. May and October offer a more intimate atmosphere, slightly cooler temperatures, and exceptional value compared to peak season, while still providing the beauty and warmth that makes Salento magical.

Yes. Many of our international couples plan multi-day celebrations, typically incorporating a welcome dinner the evening before the wedding and a relaxed farewell brunch or boat trip the morning after. We can organise the full programme across multiple days, coordinating with local accommodation providers, boat charter companies for Adriatic excursions, thermal spa experiences in Santa Cesarea Terme, and other unique experiences in the area.

Please let us know from the outset if you are considering a multi-day event so we can plan the logistics accordingly and ensure your guests have an unforgettable experience throughout their stay in Salento.

International and destination weddings are at the heart of what we do at Cala dei Balcani. Since 2003, we have welcomed couples from across the United Kingdom, Ireland, France, Germany, Spain, the Netherlands, Belgium, the United States, Canada, Australia, and beyond. Our team is fully multilingual — Italian, English, French, Spanish, and German — and experienced in managing all aspects of an international wedding remotely, including documentation support, supplier coordination, and guest logistics.

We understand the particular needs and anxieties of couples planning a wedding in a country that is not their own. We are here to make the process feel as seamless and enjoyable as possible.

Our kitchen team is experienced in accommodating the full spectrum of dietary requirements, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, shellfish-free, and other allergy-related needs. The Salentine culinary tradition is naturally rich in outstanding plant-based and pescatarian options that require little adaptation.
